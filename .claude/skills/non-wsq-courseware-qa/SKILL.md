# Non-WSQ Courseware QA

1. Confirm PPT, Learner Guide DOCX/Markdown, Lesson Plan and at least 20 lab files exist.
2. Confirm the PPT exceeds 100 slides and the Learner Guide contains detailed prompts, commands, expected results, troubleshooting and recovery for every lab.
3. Confirm course title, code, topic order and total instructional duration agree across artifacts.
4. Confirm every lab follows Frame, Plan, Generate, Inspect, Verify, Correct and Commit, plus an independent challenge and reflection.
5. Search learner deliverables for WSQ funding, SSG, TRAQOM and assessment content; report file and line.
6. Confirm every custom skill, command, hook and agent filename begins `non-wsq-` and no existing WSQ artifact changed.
7. Open and render PPT/DOCX files; inspect representative early, middle and late pages for overflow, clipping, blank pages and unreadable contrast.
8. Report pass/fail with exact artifact counts and actionable locations.
